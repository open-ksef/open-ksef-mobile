## How to Sign

Signing is automatic: when you open a pull request, the CLA bot will post a
comment asking you to sign. Reply with the exact text shown by the bot to
record your signature.

Your signature is stored in this repository under
`.github/cla-signatures.json` (on the `cla-signatures` branch).
